Skill Development camp focuses on a variety of skills including power skating, shooting stickhandling, passing and incorporates all skills into different combination drills. The day consists of two 1.5 hour ice sessions and two 1 hour off-ice training sessions. The off-ice takes place on our turf field and will focus on balance, coordination, agility and quick feet. All players bring a packed lunch and snacks for the day.
